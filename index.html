Business question
How can we identify high-risk borrowers before loan approval?

Tools
Python, Pandas, SQL, Power BI

What I did
Cleaned applicant data, analysed credit score distributions, and classified borrowers into risk categories.

Outcome
Created a risk segmentation framework to support approval decisions.
